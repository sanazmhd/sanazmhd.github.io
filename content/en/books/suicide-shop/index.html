📚Suicide-shop

April 17, 2024 · 1 min

I read The Suicide Shop by Jean Teulé, and I really enjoyed it. At first, the idea of a shop that sells things for people who want to die sounds very dark and strange. But the book is actually funny in a very unusual way. It’s called dark comedy — where serious topics are shown in a funny or strange light.

Even though the story starts very sad and negative, it slowly changes. As you read more, you start to feel that maybe there is still hope, even in a dark world. That made me like the book even more.

The characters are strange but interesting. The way they talk and act makes you think about life, sadness, and happiness. It’s not a normal story, but that’s what makes it special.

If you like stories that are a little weird, a little funny, and also make you think about life, I think you will really like this book.
